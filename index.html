POKEMONS

Arceus

Arceus is a Normal-type Mythical Pokémon. Arceus can change type and form when it is holding a Plate or type-specific Z-Crystal.

Dusknoir

Dusknoir is a Ghost-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It evolves from Dusclops when traded while holding a Reaper Cloth. It is the final form of Duskull.

Blaziken

Blaziken is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves from Combusken starting at level 36. It is the final form of Torchic.

Carnivine

Carnivine is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV.It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon.

Charizard

Charizard is a dual-type Fire/Flying Pokémon. It evolves from Charmeleon. It is the final form of Charmander.

Delphox

Delphox is a dual-type Fire/Psychic Pokémon introduced in Generation VI. It evolves from Braixen starting at level 36. It is the final form of Fennekin.

Grapploct

Grapploct is a Fighting-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VIII. It evolves from Clobbopus when leveled up while knowing Taunt.

Grimmsnarl

Grimmsnarl is a dual-type Dark/Fairy Pokémon. It evolves from Morgrem starting at level 42. It is the final form of Impidimp.

Groudon

Groudon is a Ground-type Legendary Pokémon. He can undergo Primal Reversion and become Primal Groudon if it holds the Red Orb.

Gyarados

Gyarados is a dual-type Water/Flying Pokémon. Gyarados can Mega Evolve into Mega Gyarados using the Gyaradosite.

Krookodile

Krookodile is a dual-type Ground/Dark Pokémon introduced in Generation V. It evolves from Krokorok starting at level 40. It is the final form of Sandile.

Leafeon

Leafeon is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It evolves from Eevee when leveled up near a Moss Rock or when exposed to a Leaf Stone.

Lucario

Lucario is a dual-type Fighting/Steel Pokémon. It evolves from Riolu when leveled up with high friendship during the day.

Machamp

Machamp is a Fighting-type Pokémon introduced. It evolves from Machoke when traded or when exposed to a Linking Cord. It is the final form of Machop.

Metagross

Metagross is a dual-type Steel/Psychic pseudo-legendary Pokémon. It evolves from Metang starting at level 45. It is the final form of Beldum.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon, but can Mega Evolve into two forms: Mega Mewtwo X and Mega Mewtwo Y.

Moltres

Moltres is a dual-type Fire/Flying Legendary Pokémon.Along with Articuno and Zapdos, it is one of the three legendary birds of Kanto.

Palkia

Palkia is a dual-type Water/Dragon Legendary Pokémon. Palkia can transform into a second form, Origin Forme, while holding a Lustrous Globe.

Raikou

Raikou is an Electric-type Legendary Pokémon. Along with Entei and Suicune, it is one of the Legendary beasts resurrected by Ho-Oh after the burning of the Brass Tower.

Sceptile

Sceptile is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves from Grovyle starting at level 36. It is the final form of Treecko.

Seismitoad

Seismitoad is a dual-type Water/Ground Pokémon. It evolves from Palpitoad starting at level 36. It is the final form of Tympole.

Swellow

Swellow is a dual-type Normal/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves from Taillow starting at level 22.

Umbreon

Umbreon is a Dark-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It evolves from Eevee when leveled up with high friendship during the night.

Zapdos

Zapdos is a dual-type Electric/Flying Legendary Pokémon. Along with Articuno and Moltres, it is one of the three legendary birds of Kanto.

Zeraora

Zeraora is an Electric-type Mythical Pokémon. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon. The last Pokémon in the Alola Pokédex in Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon.
